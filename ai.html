What is AI?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, refers to machines simulating human intelligence to perform tasks like learning and problem-solving. In business, it's used for automation and data analysis.

Explore subdomains: NLP (Natural Language Processing) for text understanding, and LLM (Large Language Models) like those powering chatbots. Our video series makes it simple.
